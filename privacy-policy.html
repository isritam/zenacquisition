Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Zen Acquisition respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data with transparency, discretion, and lawful processing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, services, systems, and communications.

1. Who We Are

Zen Acquisition is a client acquisition agency registered in West Bengal, India (UDYAM-WB-18-0184983), providing outbound lead generation, appointment setting, and business growth systems for agencies, consultants, and service businesses.

2. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all website visitors, prospective clients, current clients, strategic partners, and any individuals whose data is processed through our systems in connection with our services.

3. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Company or business name
  • Website, social profiles, or business assets
  • Booking details, onboarding forms, call notes, and communication records

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and browser/device type
  • General geographic location
  • Website analytics, cookies, and usage behavior

4. How We Use Information

  • Responding to inquiries and consultation requests
  • Delivering contracted services
  • Campaign execution and reporting
  • Internal business operations and optimization
  • Fraud prevention and legal compliance

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data under lawful business purposes, contractual necessity, consent, and compliance obligations under applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

6. Client Campaign Data Processing

For outbound campaigns, Zen Acquisition may act as a Data Processor on behalf of clients. Data accessed for campaign fulfillment is used solely within the agreed scope, never repurposed for unrelated campaigns, and is deleted, returned, or archived securely after engagement completion.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may only be shared with trusted service providers, software platforms, or legal authorities when operationally necessary or legally required.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as operationally necessary, contractually relevant, or legally required. Campaign communication records may be retained temporarily for quality assurance, dispute resolution, and compliance purposes.

9. Security Measures

We implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. While no system is completely immune to risk, security remains a continuous priority.

10. International Transfers

If our tools, vendors, or systems process information outside India, we take commercially appropriate steps to ensure reasonable safeguards are maintained.

11. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, objection, or withdrawal of consent regarding your personal data.

12. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies, analytics tools, and performance technologies to improve functionality, measure traffic, and enhance user experience. You may manage cookie settings through your browser.

13. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

14. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
